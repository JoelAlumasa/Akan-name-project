Akan names

Akan names vary depending on the day of birth. (M) is male and (F) is female:

  1. Sunday: Kwasi(M), Akousua(F)
  2. Monday: Kwadwo(M), Adwoa(F)
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena(M), Abenaa(F)
  4. Wednesday:Kwaku(M), Akua(F)
  5. Thursday: Yaw(M), Yaa(F)
  6. Friday: Kofi(M), Afua(F)
  7. Saturday:Kwame(M), Ama(F)
